1. Introduction

MATCHFONE is a live video matching application that allows adults to connect with other users through real-time video conversations. This Privacy Policy explains how MATCHFONE collects, uses, stores, processes, protects and deletes personal data when you use the application.

By using MATCHFONE, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

2. Age Restriction

MATCHFONE is strictly intended for users aged 18 years or older. Users must confirm that they are at least 18 years old before using the service. Individuals under 18 years of age are not permitted to use MATCHFONE.

3. Anonymous Account and User Identifier

MATCHFONE does not require users to register with an email address, phone number, Apple ID, Google account or real name. However, MATCHFONE automatically creates an anonymous technical account using Supabase Authentication.

This anonymous account generates a unique user identifier, also known as a UUID. This identifier is used to operate the service, enable matching, process reports and blocks, enforce moderation actions and maintain platform safety.

The anonymous account is not a public profile and does not contain your name, email address or phone number.

4. Data We Collect

4.1 Anonymous User Data

  • Anonymous user identifier
  • Authentication session information
  • User status
  • Age confirmation status
  • Ban or restriction status
  • Last activity timestamp

4.2 Camera and Microphone Data

MATCHFONE requires access to your camera and microphone to enable live video calls. Video and audio are transmitted in real time through live communication infrastructure.

MATCHFONE does not record video calls. MATCHFONE does not store live audio or video recordings.

4.3 Face Check and Age Verification

MATCHFONE uses an on-device Face Check process to help verify that a real person is present and to support the adult-only nature of the service. This verification runs locally on your device.

MATCHFONE does not upload or store face images, biometric templates, face embeddings or facial recognition profiles. Only a local verification result may be stored on your device to remember that the Face Check was completed.

4.4 Matching and Connection Data

To provide matching functionality, MATCHFONE may process and store:

  • Match identifiers
  • Anonymous user identifiers of matched users
  • Connection status
  • Start and end timestamps
  • Queue status
  • Technical call information

4.5 Reports

Users may report other users for safety reasons. When a report is submitted, MATCHFONE may store:

  • Reporting user identifier
  • Reported user identifier
  • Match identifier
  • Report reason
  • Optional report details
  • Report timestamp
  • Report status

4.6 Blocks

Users may block other users. MATCHFONE stores block records so that blocked users are not matched again where technically possible.

4.7 Moderation Events

MATCHFONE uses automated moderation systems to help detect prohibited content and unsafe behavior. Moderation may analyze selected video frames during a live call.

Moderation may detect categories such as:

  • Nudity
  • Sexual content or sexual behavior
  • Weapons
  • Drug-related content
  • Violence
  • Unsafe or prohibited conduct

MATCHFONE may store moderation results, including category, confidence score, source, timestamp and enforcement action. MATCHFONE does not permanently store the analyzed video frame itself.

4.8 Bans and Enforcement Records

MATCHFONE may store ban and enforcement records when users violate the rules. This may include the anonymous user identifier, reason, timestamp and enforcement status.

5. Data We Do Not Collect

MATCHFONE does not intentionally collect:

  • Real names
  • Email addresses from users inside the app
  • Phone numbers
  • Postal addresses
  • Payment information
  • Government identification documents
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device fingerprinting data
  • Stored biometric templates

6. Purposes of Processing

MATCHFONE processes data for the following purposes:

  • Providing live video matching functionality
  • Creating and maintaining anonymous user sessions
  • Operating real-time video connections
  • Enforcing the 18+ requirement
  • Preventing abuse and unsafe behavior
  • Processing reports and blocks
  • Applying warnings, restrictions or bans
  • Maintaining platform security
  • Complying with legal obligations

Automated moderation is used to maintain safety in real time. Users may contact MATCHFONE if they believe an enforcement action was applied incorrectly.

Where deletion is requested, MATCHFONE will delete or anonymize personal data unless retention is required for legal, security, fraud prevention or safety reasons.
